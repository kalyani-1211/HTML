Computer Science(CSE):

A comprehensive program that prepares students for careers in technology, programming, and software development.Computer Science (CS) also integrated as Electrical engineering and Computer Science (EECS) in some universities, is an academic program at many universities which comprises approaches of computer science and computer engineering. There is no clear division in computing between science and engineering, just like in the field of materials science and engineering.

Electronics and Communication(ECE):

Equipping students with the necessary skills to manage and lead businesses in the global economy.lectronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) course falls under the purview of Engineering field, which includes detailed analytical study about the design, operation, production, and maintenance related knowledge of electronics and communication systems. ECE courses can be pursued at various levels such as Diploma, UG, and PG, which are offered by several popular universities and colleges in India. Universities keep on incorporating new subjects to their syllabus, such as the Internet of Things or IoT, in order to make the students future job-ready.
